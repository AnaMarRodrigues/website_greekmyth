    • Welcome to Olympian Roots,

    Have you ever wondered about the relationships of the Greek gods or been fascinated by their mythical world?

    This project is the result of my master’s research, creating an interactive encyclopedia of Greek mythology. It helps users explore the gods’ lives, stories, and connections in an engaging and easy-to-understand way. With god designs made by me, with thought and mantaining the symbols and core of each god.
    Whether you are a student, enthusiast, or casual explorer, this digital encyclopedia invites you to connect with these timeless stories in a meaningful way.
